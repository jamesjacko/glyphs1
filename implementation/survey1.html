Glyph Based Visualisation Study

We have created a system which generates glyph small, discrete, visualisations (glyphs). This study will allow us to evaluate the effectiveness of the generated glyphs. The study is split into three parts:

  1. Evalute the glyphs effectiveness of depicting data 1. You will be asked to differentiate depictions of small values against those with high values.
  2. Evalute the glyphs effectiveness of depicting data 2. You will be asked to order sequence of glyphs from low to high.
  3. Evalute your feelings towards each glyph type. You will be asked to rate a particular glypoh based on its appeal and ability to portray the information.
