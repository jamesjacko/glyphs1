Glyph Based Visualisation Study

Please read the following carefully.

The Visualisation Modelling Graphics research group in Bangor University is working on a system for producing micro visualisations using a novel technique. You can find out more about the study here.

We have created a system which generates small, discrete, visualisations. This study will allow us to evaluate the effectiveness of the generated visualisations. The study is split into three parts:

  1. Evalute the visualisations effectiveness of depicting data 1. You will be asked to differentiate depictions of small values against those with high values.
  2. Evalute the visualisations effectiveness of depicting data 2. You will be asked to order sequence of glyphs from low to high.
  3. You will be asked for your opinions on the visualisations that you have surveyed and your thoughts on the method as a whole.
